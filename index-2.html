About Me

Driven by Science,
Defined by Curiosity

I am a final-year Biomedical Science undergraduate with a deep passion for antimicrobial resistance research, molecular biology, and evidence-based medicine.

Currently completing my BSc (Hons) at NSBM Green University, I have developed expertise in systematic review methodology, microbiology, and scientific communication. My research focuses on the comparative multidrug resistance profiles of Pseudomonas aeruginosa and Acinetobacter baumannii in ICU settings across South Asia.

I aspire to pursue an MSc in Molecular Biology or Biotechnology internationally — with a particular interest in Australian research institutions — and contribute to global efforts in combating antimicrobial resistance.
